Begin by finding the ID to your Sleeper Fantasy League*

To find you league ID either:

On a Computer:
  1. Go to sleeper.app and find the league you want.
  2. Copy the number in the URL that comes after
    https://sleeper.app/leagues/
  3. Paste it below
 On the Sleeper App:
  1. Go to your League page
  2. Tap the settings gear by League Settings
  3. Tap on General
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Copy League ID
  5. Paste the league id into the form below
*note: SFF works for all Sleeper fantasy leagues, but it does not work for March Madness, Squads, or any other event that doesn't involve a draft or rosters.
